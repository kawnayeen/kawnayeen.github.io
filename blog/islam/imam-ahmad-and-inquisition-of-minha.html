In the Name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful

Allah says (interpretation of the meaning) :

And if any one of the polytheists seeks your protection, then grant him protection so that he may hear the words of Allah .

At-Tawbah (9:6)

Those who lagged behind will say, when you set forth to take the spoils, "Allow us to follow you," They want to change Allah's Words. Say: "You shall not follow us; thus Allah has said beforehand." Then they will say: "Nay, you envy us." Nay, but they understand not except a little.

Al-Fath (48:15)

Imam Ahmad related that Jabir Ibn 'Abdullah narrated:

Allah's Messenger would offer himself to the people at each station of pilgrimage. He would say: 'Is there anyone who would take me with him to his people for the Quraish have objected that I convey the words of my Lord.' ...

Musnad Ahmad No. 14770, Tirmidhi 2925, Abu Dawood 4734

This is from the core belief of Muslims that Al-Quran is the word of Allah. Now if someone say that Quran is created (may Allah safeguard us from saying such), that imply Allah created his word. In other word, it is equivalent to say Allah created his own attribute and making such statement is clear act of disbelief.

They went to the extreme of imprisoning & torturing those righteous scholars who rejected to accept such innovation. Imam Ahmad Ibn Hanbal was one of the scholars who were persecuted.

When Imam Ahmad was brought in the court of Caliph Mu'tasim, he refuted all the logic of deviant Mutazila scholars infront of the people of Baghdad. When Mutazilaite scholars were defeated in the debate by Imam Ahmad & failed to bring any single proof from Quran and Sunnah to backup their claim, then Mu'tasim start lashing Imam Ahmad.

I'll not go in details of the persecution of Imam Ahmad, amount and way of lashing the oppressor did to Imam Ahmad. Interested reader can check out the 10th volume of Al-Bidaya wan-Nahiya of Ibn Katheer. The point is, Imam Ahmad refused to accept deviant ideology, Imam Ahmad refused to utter word of disbelief; no matter how severe the lashing and persecution is. People of Baghdad was watching what Imam Ahmad do & He safeguarded The Belief of Muslim infront of them, He safeguarded the religion for the later generations to come. And Allah safeguarded his name and his legacy. May Allah have mercy on Imam Ahmad Ibn Hanbal.
